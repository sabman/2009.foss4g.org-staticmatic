Presentations sessions provide you with a platform to highlight your experiences.

As a presenter you are expected to provide a digital copy of your presentation.

Presentations

94 presentations are scheduled from the 21st to the 23rd October. Each session runs for 25 minutes. The following is a summary of the presentation schedule

  • October 21st - 30 Sessions across 5 rooms

  • October 22nd - 36 Sessions across 3 rooms

  • October 23rd - 31 Sessions across 5 rooms
